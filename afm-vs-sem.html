Key Differences

  • Dimensionality SEM is 2D projection. AFM is true 3D.
  • Environment SEM needs Vacuum. AFM works in Air/Liquid.
  • Preparation SEM needs conductive coating. AFM needs minimal prep.

Visual Comparison

Notice how the SEM image looks like a photograph with shadows, but lacks height data. The AFM image provides precise Z-axis measurements.

Resolution Limits

  • SEM: ~0.5 nm (Lateral)
  • AFM: ~0.1 nm (Lateral & Vertical)
