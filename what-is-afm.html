The Feedback Loop

  • 1
    Tip Scan Sharp tip moves over atoms
  • 2
    Cantilever Flex Surface forces bend the lever
  • 3
    Laser Deflection Laser beam moves on detector
  • 4
    Correction (Z) Piezo moves to maintain force

Key Components

  • Cantilever & Tip: The "finger" that feels the surface.
  • Laser & PSD: Optical lever system to detect nanometer movements.
  • Piezo Scanner: Extremely precise motors for XYZ movement.
