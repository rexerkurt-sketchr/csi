Soft SThM Logic

  • 1. Heated Tip Probe is maintained at high T.
  • 2. Gentle Touch Intermittent Contact prevents surface melt/drag.
  • 3. Heat Flux Heat flows from tip to sample on contact.

Thermal Imaging

Standard SThM drags a hot tip, which can melt polymers. Soft SThM hops, measuring thermal conductivity without damaging the sample.
